About Us

We are a local tee shop built around soft fabrics, bold art, and friendly service. Everything we print is made to be worn on repeat.

Who we are

Oak Street Tee Co. started as a weekend pop-up with a few graphic tees and a borrowed screen. Today we are a Riverside storefront that prints locally, collaborates with artists, and keeps a rotating shelf of premium basics.

Why local matters

When you stop by, you meet the people who designed the graphics and pulled the prints. We build collections that reflect our neighborhood, our music, and our culture, and we keep sizes inclusive so everyone feels at home.

Quality you can feel

We choose heavyweight cotton, soft tri-blends, and water-based inks that stay smooth over time. Our team fits every style and washes test pieces so each tee feels right from day one.
